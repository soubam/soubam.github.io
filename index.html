About Me

I am a PhD student at IIIT Delhi. I am working under the guidance of Vinayak Naik and Dipanjan Chakraborty. My research area is Human Activity Recognition using Smartphone and Wearable Sensors.

I completed my BE in Computer Science from Anna University, Coimbatore. I did my MTech in Computer Science with specialization in Privacy and Security from IIIT-D
