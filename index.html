About Me

I am a first year PhD student at Mobile and Ubiquitous Computing (MUC) group, IIIT Delhi. I am working under the guidance of Dr. Vinayak Naik and Dr. Dipanjan Chakorbaty. My research area is Participatory Sensing using Mobile Phone Sensors.

I completed my BE in Computer Science from Anna University, Coimbatore. I did my MTech in Computer Science with specialization in Privacy and Security from IIIT-D
