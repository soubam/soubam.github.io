Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Any student in IIIT Delhi with a valid IIITD email account can particpate.

Device Requirement

You should have an Android device with Android 3.0 or higher and it should be connected to Institute's WiFi network.

Do I need a data plan?

No. Your data will be uploaded when you are connected to IIITD WiFi.

What are the prizes?

50 top data collectors for the month will be given Flipkart vouchers worth Rs. 200 each.

What are the data being collected?

  • Accelerometer
  • WiFi Access Point information
  • Google Activity Recogntion API data

Data Privacy

The data will be used only for research purpose. User anonimity will be maintained. No data will be published without prior permission from the volunteer.
